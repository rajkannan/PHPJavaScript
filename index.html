Having started operations in 2005, Divine Arts and Crafts is known for the consistently high quality of its offerings, each of which is hand-picked by its team.

Some of the qualities which we look for in our artworks include high aesthetic value and quality of the material.

Not only do we love the rich, living spiritual and cultural traditions of India, the aim has also been to share a fair amount of our profits with the numerous highly skilled artists scattered all over the subcontinent.

Over the years, Divine Arts and Crafts has been satisfying customers worldwide with good amount of hand-picked product range which include statues from the subcontinent, paintings from India, and some decorative items. We have been involved in gathering various arts and craft items from the Indian subcontinent gaining in the process the confidence of our valuable patrons, and more importantly, their affection as well.

Our Mission: To make Indian subcontinent's arts, crafts, and spiritual depth accessible to all.
